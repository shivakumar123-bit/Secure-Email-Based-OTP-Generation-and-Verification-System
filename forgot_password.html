How to Create a Gmail App Password

  1. Go to Google Account and sign in.
  2. Click on "Security" in the left sidebar.
  3. Ensure that 2-Step Verification is turned on.
  4. Once enabled, click on "App Passwords".
  5. Select the app (Mail) and device (Other) then click "Generate".
  6. Copy the generated App Password.

After generating your App Password, return to the OTP page and paste it.
